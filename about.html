// The Mission

Breaking Things to Understand Them

Bug bounty hunting is the art of legally finding security vulnerabilities in real-world applications and getting paid for it. But before you can break things responsibly, you have to understand how they're built. This site exists to document that learning process ‚Äî from foundational concepts to hands-on hacking practice ‚Äî and share everything discovered along the way.

// The Approach

One Concept at a Time

Cybersecurity is a massive field, and trying to learn all of it at once leads nowhere fast. The approach here is deliberate and sequential: build a solid base in networking and web development, layer on security fundamentals, and steadily work toward real vulnerability research. Progress over perfection, always.
